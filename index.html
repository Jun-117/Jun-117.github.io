How it works

Three steps to water you can trust

No jars to order. No boiling. No filter guesswork. KORA CARE runs your home's drinking water as a service.

1

We install

A multi-stage purifier, fitted in your kitchen by our local team. Before anything else, we test your tap water in front of you — and again after installation, so you see the difference.

2

You drink

Unlimited purified water from your own tap, every day. The equipment stays under KORA CARE's care — you simply subscribe month to month.

3

We care

Every month our team visits your home: filters checked and replaced on schedule, water quality re-tested, everything kept in top condition. That's the CARE in KORA CARE.
